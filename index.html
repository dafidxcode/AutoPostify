Facebook Media Uploader

Upload your reels, videos, and images directly to Facebook Pages with ease!

What is this?

A simple tool to help you schedule and upload media content to your Facebook Page automatically.

How to Use

1. Enter your Page ID and Access Token
2. Select media type (Reel/Video/Image)
3. Choose your file and add details
4. Schedule or upload immediately

Scheduling

Set your preferred upload time in WIB timezone. Your content will be automatically published at the scheduled time.
